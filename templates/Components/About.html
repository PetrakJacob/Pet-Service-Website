📖 History

Founded in 2010 by pet care professional Emily Jones, Happy Hounds began as an one-woman dog walking and sitting service. As the business quickly gained a reputation for reliable and trustworthy pet care, Emily recruited a team of passionate and experienced dog walkers and sitters. Today, Happy Hounds has expanded to offer a full range of dog care services: including doggie daycare, boarding and grooming. Despite its growth, Happy Hounds remains dedicated to providing personalized, compassionate care to all its clients.
